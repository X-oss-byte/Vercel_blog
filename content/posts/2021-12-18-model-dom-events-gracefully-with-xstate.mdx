DOM is an inevitable part of working with Javascript in the browser. If you've built any web application, you've definitely worked with the DOM either directly or through abstractions such as JSX.

The way the users of your application can interact with your application is through the DOM events. Events such as click, focus, blur, keydown, keyup, etc. are the events that are fired by the user.
